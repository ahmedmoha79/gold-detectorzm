Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Garrett metal detectors, shipping, warranty, and using them in Zambian conditions.

Which Garrett detector is best for Zambian mineralized soils?

The Garrett Axiom is specifically designed for highly mineralized soils like those found in Zambia's goldfields. Its pulse induction technology and advanced ground balancing make it the top choice. For less mineralized areas or smaller gold, the Garrett Goldmaster offers excellent VLF performance.

Do you offer training on how to use the detectors?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training with every detector purchase. Our experts will show you how to set up your detector, understand the signals, and use it effectively in Zambian conditions. We also offer advanced training sessions for experienced users.

What warranty do Garrett detectors come with?

All Garrett metal detectors sold through our authorized dealership come with a 3-year manufacturer warranty. This covers defects in materials and workmanship. We also provide local support and can facilitate warranty claims if needed.

Do you deliver across Zambia?

Yes, we deliver to all major towns and cities across Zambia. Delivery times and costs vary depending on location. Contact us with your location for specific delivery information. We also offer pickup from our Lusaka location.

How does pulse induction differ from VLF technology?

Pulse Induction (PI) detectors like the Garrett Axiom send powerful, short bursts of current through a coil, making them excellent for highly mineralized soils and saltwater environments. VLF detectors use continuous sine waves and are generally more sensitive to small gold but can be affected by mineralization. For Zambia's challenging soils, PI technology typically performs better.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. For larger purchases, we can arrange payment plans. All prices are in Zambian Kwacha (ZMW). Contact us to discuss payment options that work for you.

How long does the battery last on Garrett detectors?

Battery life varies by model. The Garrett Axiom typically provides 12-16 hours of continuous use on a single charge. The Goldmaster and Scanner models offer 20+ hours. All come with rechargeable battery systems and chargers.

Do you have spare coils and accessories in stock?

Yes, we maintain stock of popular Garrett coils and accessories including headphones, covers, and digging tools. If you need a specific item not in stock, we can typically source it within 2-4 weeks.
